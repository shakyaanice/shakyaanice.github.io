About Me

I am a Geospatial Research Associate working at Geoinformatics Center - Asian Institute of Technology (GIC-AIT). My research interests include Geographic Information System (GIS), Remote Sensing (RS), hazard modelling, disaster risk reduction and multi-hazard risk assessment. I am keen to apply programming into hazard and risk assessment. I am also interested in cartography and illustration.
